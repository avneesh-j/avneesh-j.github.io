Interests

As a devoted English Language Lecturer, my interests encompass a profound love for literature, delving into the rich tapestry of literary genres, historical epochs, and cultural narratives. The magic of language captivates me, inspiring a keen exploration of linguistic principles, syntax intricacies, phonetics, and etymology. Equipped with these passions, I strive to infuse my teaching with enthusiasm, fostering critical analysis and a love for the English language, aiming to inspire the next generation of language enthusiasts.
